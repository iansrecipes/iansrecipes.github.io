Birthday Cake Recipe

Ingredients:

Dry:

  • 2/3 cup Wholesome Foods organic brown sugar
  • 2 tsp Hain's Featherweight aluminum-free/gluten-free baking powder
  • 1 tsp Bob's Red Mill xanthan gum
  • 2 tsp unflavored gelatin
  • 1/3 cup Bob's Red Mill organic amaranth flour
  • 1/3 cup Bob's Red Mill organic coconut flour
  • 1/3 cup Bob's Red Mill tapioca flour
  • 1/3 cup Bob's Red Mill potato starch flour

Wet:

  • 1/3 cup Spectrum Naturals organic shortening
  • 1 tsp Flavorganics gluten-free vanilla
  • 3 organic brown eggs (or substitute)
  • 2/3 cup "milk" (1/3 cup Thai Kitchen organic coconut milk and 1/3 cup water)
  • 2 Tbsp water

    • Directions:


    1. Using mixer, combine all dry ingredients together.
    2. Add shortening and blend in completely.
    3. Add "milk," eggs or substitute, and vanilla.
    4. Try not to over mix as xanthan gum can get pretty gummy. If you need to add the extra water, do it one tablespoon at a time. This dough is thicker, like a muffin batter, not runny.
    5. Grease and amaranth flour a 9-inch cake pan. This batter does great as cupcakes or small novelty shape cakes.
    6. Bake for 35 minutes at 350 degrees. 20 minutes for cupcakes/novelty size cakes.
    7. Cool on rack for 5-10 minutes before removing cake from pan.


    Note: I always make this cake fresh the day of the celebration. It just tastes so much better fresh. Once cool I sprinkle with powdered sugar, use fresh fruit, or accent with minimal frosting. Once cool, be sure to cover and refrigerate any unused cake for later.

    Chocolate Cake: Increase brown sugar to 1 cup and add 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder to the flours.
