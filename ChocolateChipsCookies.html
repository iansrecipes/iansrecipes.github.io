Ingredients:


Dry:


  • 3/4 cup Wholesome Foods organic brown sugar
  • 2 tsp Hain's Featherweight aluminum-free/gluten-free baking powder
  • 1 tsp Bob's Red Mill xanthan gum
  • 1 tsp Redmond's sea salt
  • 1 cup Bob's Red Mill organic amaranth flour
  • 1 cup Bob's Red Mill organic coconut flour
  • 3/4 cup Bob's Red Mill tapioca flour
  • 1/2 cup Bob's Red Mill potato starch flour
  • 2 cups Sunspire organic semi-sweet chocolate chips


Wet:

  • 1/2 cup Spectrum Naturals organic shortening
  • 1 stick Horizon organic butter
  • 1 tsp Flavorganics gluten-free vanilla
  • 2 organic eggs

Directions:
1. Preheat oven to 375-degrees.
2. In a mixing bowl, beat the shortening and butter on medium speed for about 30 seconds.
3. Add brown sugar, cane sugar, eggs, vanilla, baking powder, amaranth flour and coconut flour. Beat until creamy and thoroughly combined.
4. Beat in the tapioca flour, potato starch flour, xanthan gum, and salt.
5. With a spoon, stir in chocolate chips and/or chopped nuts.
6. Drop the dough by rounded teaspoonfuls, about 2 inches apart, on an ungreased cookie sheet. Bake in 375-degree oven for 9-12 minutes or until the edges are lightly browned.
7. Remove cookies immediately and place on wire cooling rack. Once cool, store in an airtight container.
8. If you add some cocoa powder to the dry ingredients, you will have chocolate-chocolate chip cookies!
