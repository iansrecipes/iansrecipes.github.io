Banana Bread Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup coconut flour
  • 1/2 cup amaranth flour
  • 1/2 cup tapioca flour
  • 1/2 cup potato starch flour
  • 2/3 cup sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon xanthan gum
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/3 cup shortening
  • 4 tablespoons milk (touch of coconut milk & water)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup mashed bananas (2-3 medium)
  • 1/3 cup chopped nuts (optional)

    • Directions:
    1. In a large mixing bowl combine coconut flour, amaranth flour, tapioca flour, potato starch flour, sugar, baking powder, xanthan gum, baking soda, and salt. Cut in shortening with beaters until mixture is crumbly.
    2. Put in 2 tablespoons milk, 1 egg , 1 banana and lightly beat to combine. Then add remaining 2 tablespoons milk, 1 egg, remaining bananas and beat to combine. Donâ€™t over beat. Add nuts if desired.
    3. Pour into 2 greased and floured loaf pans. Bake at 375 degrees for 35 minutes.
    4. Cool for 10 minutes on wire rack. Remove from pans. Cool completely before slicing or placing in plastic bag. Store in refrigerator or freeze.

    Note: Can substitute 1 cup of rice flour for coconut/amaranth combination. Download this recipe
