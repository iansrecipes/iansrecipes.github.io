Gingerbread Cookies



  • 3/4 cup ghee
  • 1/4 cup molasses
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 Tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tsp xanthan gum
  • 1-1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tsp ginger
  • 1/2 tsp cloves
  • 2-1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1-1/2 cup amaranth flour
  • 1 cup tapioca flour
  • 1 cup potato starch flour
  • Sugar to sprinkle on top
  1. Beat medium to high together ghee, molasses, brown sugar, egg and vinegar.
  2. Add xanthan gum, salt, cinnamon, ginger, cloves, and baking powder. Beat to combine. Add the flours 1/2 Cup at a time, beating after each.
  3. Cool dough in fridge 1-3 hours or until easy to handle.
  4. On lightly floured surface, roll half the dough to 1/4 inch thick. Cut out.
  5. Place one inch apart on lightly oiled cook sheet. Sprinkle with sugar.
  6. Use raisins or cranberries to eyes and buttons
  7. Bake at 375 degrees for 12-15 minutes.
  8. Immediately remove cookies to cooling rack to prevent them from breaking.
